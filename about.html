About NovaEcoSort

NovaEcoSort is an AI-powered waste classification system that helps users identify different types of waste through image analysis. Our goal is to encourage proper waste segregation, recycling, and sustainable waste management using Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make waste segregation simple and accessible for everyone. By combining AI and modern web technologies, NovaEcoSort promotes eco-friendly habits and supports a cleaner environment.
